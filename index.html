About the project

The purpose of this project was to demonstrate the knowledge and techniques I learned about Data Visualization this semester. Out of personal interest, I decided to study a dataset of the last ~1000 fights in the UFC (Ultimate Fighting Championship).

My focus during this project ended up on the statistical advantage (or lack thereof) that fighters could have based on the difference between their age and that of their opponent. Using three distinct types of visualizations, I have displayed my findings, so enjoy!
